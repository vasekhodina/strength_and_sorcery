Welcome to the Strength and Sorcery game page

Strength and sorcery is a classic tiled dungeon game, taking inspiration from games like Dungeon Master, Might and Magic (see what I did there?) and other. For now you could say it's in really early alpha, basically it's just one level dungeon walking simulator. Hopefully more features are to come.
